Investment Proposal for TradeVista

Dear Investor,

I am excited to present an opportunity to invest in TradeVista, a groundbreaking platform designed to connect business owners with potential buyers. 1. Clear Problem-Solution Fit

The Problem: The high unemployment rate in our country remains a significant challenge, along with limited opportunities for small businesses and service providers to showcase their offerings effectively.

Our Solution: TradeVista bridges this gap by creating a robust e-commerce platform where users can:

  • Register their businesses and showcase their offerings.
  • Post videos and images to enhance product/service visibility.
  • Leverage tools like account verification, promotional activities, and sponsored posts to gain more visibility and credibility.

TradeVista empowers businesses and service providers, creating jobs and stimulating economic growth.

2. Market Validation

TradeVista operates in the thriving e-commerce industry, an open and inclusive market with significant growth potential. Unlike competitors such as Jumia and GG, TradeVista differentiates itself by:

  • Offering advanced tools for business owners to enhance visibility.
  • Providing account verification for credibility.
  • Enabling promotion through sponsored posts and other features.

3. Technology & Innovation

TradeVista is built using advanced programming languages like PHP and JavaScript, ensuring a robust and scalable foundation. The platform is designed to be user-friendly, adaptable, and secure, allowing seamless navigation for users.

4. Strong Revenue Model

TradeVista has a sustainable and lucrative revenue model, with income generated from:

  • Account verification fees.
  • Sponsored post promotions.
  • Unlocking advanced tools on a weekly or monthly subscription basis.

We project a minimum net profit of ₦80 million Naira per annum.

5. Investment Details

We seek an investment of ₦3 million Naira, in return for which the investor will receive 10% of net profit for the next 5 years.

Mitigation Plans: TradeVista will continuously innovate by introducing new tools, enhancing platform security, and employing targeted marketing strategies to stay ahead of competitors.
